5. Working Hours and Attendance

5.1 Working Hours

Standard working hours are Sunday to Thursday, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Employees are entitled to a one-hour lunch break. 5.2 Overtime

Overtime must be pre-approved by management. Employees will be compensated for overtime in accordance with Egyptian labor laws. 5.3 Attendance and Punctuality

Employees are expected to be punctual and adhere to their work schedules.

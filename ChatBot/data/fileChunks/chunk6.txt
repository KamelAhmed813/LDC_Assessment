4.3 Maternity and Paternity Leave

Female employees are entitled to 90 days of paid maternity leave. Male employees are entitled to five days of paid paternity leave. 4.4 Public Holidays

Employees are entitled to all public holidays recognized by the Egyptian government. These include, but are not limited to:

Eid al-Fitr

Eid al-Adha

Revolution Day (July 23rd)

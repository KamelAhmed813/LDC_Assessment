Bonuses are distributed at the discretion of management based on individual and company performance. 4. Leave Policies

4.1 Annual Leave

Employees are entitled to 21 days of paid annual leave per year, as per Egyptian labor law. Leave requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance and approved by the direct manager. 4.2 Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to paid sick leave upon submission of a valid medical certificate. Sick leave is capped at 30 days per year.
